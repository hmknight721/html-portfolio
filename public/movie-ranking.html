The Best Movies According to Hannah

My top 3 movies of all time.

Beauty and the Beast

Favorite Disney movie ever since I was a kid.

A League of Their Own

Wonderful sports history movie and it contains some of my favorite actors/actresses of all time.

Pretty Woman

This idea of this movie seems so scandalous but it was done in such a tasteful and heartwarming way. I just love the chemistry between Richard Gere and Julia Roberts!
